CSO and Media

1. To what extent are state institutions in Kosovo transparent and open

Half of the polled CSOs (50%) believe that the executive institutions are open and transparent to the average level and legislative institutions in high level. 41.6% of them think that judicial institutions are open to the average level, the rest are divided in opinions. While most of the polled media (85%) also believe that the executive institutions are open and transparent to the average level,but less than that (57.14%) thinks like that for legislative and judicial ones.

2. To what extent are state institutions in Kosovo accountable for their actions

In terms of responsibility half of the polled CSO (50%) believe that the legislature is responsible on average level and 41.6% at a high level.For in legislative institutions 41.6% believe that they are responsible at a high level and 33.33% on average. While the polled media share their opinion, 41% of them believe that executive, legislative and judicial institutions are responsible to the average level.

3. To what extent are state institutions in Kosovo efficient in their work

Most of the polled CSOs and media organizations ( 66.67% and 71.43% respectively) believe that executive institutions are moderately effective. While 71.43% of polled CSOs have evaluated legislative with high efficiency on their work, and 71.43% of polled media as average efficiency. A negative assessment in judicial institutions where the polled CSOs and media organizations( 33.33% and 42.86% respectively) evaluated it effective at a low and very low level.
